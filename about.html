About

Creative developer based in Amsterdam. I build digital experiences that sit at the edge of code and craft — somewhere between engineering and art direction.

My work spans UI/UX design, WebGL, motion, and brand identity. I'm drawn to projects where the interface feels like something — where every scroll, hover, and transition is considered.

Outside the screen: music shapes how I think about rhythm in animation, film shapes how I think about pacing and narrative, and the ocean reminds me that the best things take time.

Currently available for freelance — if you're building something that should feel as good as it looks, let's talk.
